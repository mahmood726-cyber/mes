About MES

v1.0.0

Multiverse Evidence Synthesis — replacing point estimates with evidence landscapes.

Methodology

MES uses a three-phase approach: ASSESS builds evidence dossiers for each study (design tier, risk of bias, publication bias profile); EXPLORE executes hundreds of analytical specifications across estimator, CI method, bias correction, quality filter, and design filter dimensions; MAP synthesizes the multiverse into concordance metrics and a robustness verdict (Robust / Moderate / Fragile / Unstable).
